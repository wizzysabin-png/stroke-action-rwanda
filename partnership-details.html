Directly funding the recovery journey of stroke survivors in Rwandan communities.

About the Program

As a Program Sponsor, your organization adopts a specific community outreach or rehabilitation clinic. This funding ensures that survivors in rural or under-served areas of Rwanda have access to specialized physical and occupational therapy.

  • Impact: Supports up to 50 survivors monthly with professional physiotherapy.
  • Visibility: Your organization‚Äôs name and logo prominently displayed at the clinic site.
  • Reporting: Receive semi-annual data on patient recovery milestones and equipment usage.
  • Engagement: Opportunities for your staff to visit and volunteer at the sponsored facility.

Why Sponsor?

Stroke recovery requires long-term commitment. By sponsoring a center, you move beyond awareness into direct medical action, providing the physical tools necessary for survivors to regain their independence.
