The Recovery Roadmap

Phase 1: Acute Recovery (Days 1-30)

Initial medical stabilization and beginning of passive range-of-motion exercises.

Phase 2: Active Rehab (Months 1-6)

Intensive therapy sessions focusing on neuroplasticity and re-training the brain.

Phase 3: Long-term Maintenance

Community support groups and home-based exercise to maintain gains.
