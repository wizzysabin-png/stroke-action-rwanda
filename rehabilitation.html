Rehabilitation Services

Recovery is a marathon, not a sprint. We provide the tools and professional guidance to help you regain your independence.

Types of Intervention

Holistic care tailored to individual needs.

💪

Physical Therapy

Focusing on mobility, strength, and balance to help survivors walk and move again.

🧠

Occupational Therapy

Re-learning daily activities like dressing, eating, and writing to foster independence.

🗣️

Speech Therapy

Assisting with communication challenges (Aphasia) and swallowing difficulties.

Recovery Journey

Phase 1:We sub acute Stroke Care (Days 1-30)

Initial medical stabilization and beginning of passive range-of-motion exercises.

Phase 2: Active Rehab (Months 1-6)

Intensive therapy sessions focusing on neuroplasticity and re-training the brain.

Phase 3: Long-term Support

Community support groups and home-based exercise to maintain gains.

Stroke Action Rwanda

Working to prevent stroke, ensure early treatment, and support long-term recovery for individuals and families across Rwanda.
