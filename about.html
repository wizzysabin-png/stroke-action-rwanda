Who We Are & We Serve

Stroke Action Rwanda is a non-profit organization dedicated to reducing the burden of stroke through prevention, treatment, rehabilitation, and long-term support.


We serve-Individuals at risk of stroke, stroke survivors, caregivers, health professionals, and communities across Rwanda.

Our Mission

To combat stroke through prevention, effective treatment, and comprehensive long-term care and support.

Our Vision

A Rwanda where stroke is prevented whenever possible and every person affected by stroke has access to timely care, rehabilitation, and long-term support.

What We Do

  • • Stroke prevention and education
  • • Community awareness campaigns
  • • Support for stroke survivors and caregivers
  • • Advocacy and partnerships with health professionals
